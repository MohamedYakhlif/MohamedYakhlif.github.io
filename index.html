Our Services

Hematology

Complete blood count, differential counts, coagulation studies, and other blood-related diagnostics to assess overall health and detect various conditions.

Biochemistry

Comprehensive analysis of blood chemistry parameters including glucose, cholesterol, kidney function, liver function, and electrolytes for metabolic assessment.

Immunology

Evaluation of immune system components and functions to diagnose autoimmune disorders, allergies, and immunodeficiencies.

Bacteriology

Identification of bacterial infections through cultures, sensitivity testing, and microscopic examination to guide appropriate treatment.

Virology & Mycology

Detection and analysis of viral infections and fungal diseases through modern diagnostic techniques and specialized testing.

Parasitology

Identification and examination of parasitic infections through stool analysis, blood tests, and other specialized diagnostic methods.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I prepare for blood tests?

Many blood tests require fasting for 8-12 hours prior to collection. This means no food or drinks except water. For specific tests like glucose tolerance tests or lipid profiles, special preparation may be needed. Always follow the instructions provided by your doctor or our laboratory staff.

How long does it take to get test results?

Most routine tests are available within 24-48 hours. Some specialized tests may take longer, typically 3-7 days. For urgent tests, we offer expedited processing with results available within a few hours. You can receive your results in person, by email, or through our secure online portal.

Do you offer home sample collection?

Yes, we provide home sample collection services for patients who are unable to visit our laboratory. This service is available 7 days a week. Please call us at 06 28 83 65 01 to schedule a home visit with our qualified phlebotomist.

Do I need an appointment for laboratory tests?

While appointments are not mandatory for routine tests, we recommend scheduling an appointment to minimize waiting time. For specialized tests or during peak hours, appointments are advisable. You can schedule an appointment by phone or through our website.

Are your tests covered by insurance?

Most of our tests are covered by major health insurance providers in Morocco. We also work with CNOPS, CNSS, and private insurance companies. Please bring your insurance card and any necessary authorization forms. Our administrative staff will assist you with insurance claims and documentation.
